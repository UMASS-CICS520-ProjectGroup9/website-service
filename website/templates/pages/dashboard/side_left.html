Ultimate Map for Academic Strategy & Success (UMASS)

About

Ultimate Map for Academic Strategy & Success (UMASS) is a comprehensive web platform designed to help university students thrive academically and prepare for their future careers. Students search for information which comes from the office or students may share resources, access course information, evaluate professors, collaborate with peers, and strategize their academic journey.
